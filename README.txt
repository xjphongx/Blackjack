Blackjack Project

Object of the Game
    Each player attempts to beat the dealer by getting as close to 21 as possible, without going over.

Special Card Values
    Ace worth 1 or 11
    Face cards are worth 10
    Any other card is its pip value

Betting
    Testing with fake bets

Shuffle and Cut
    Dealer will shuffle all 6 decks until all the cards have been mixed. 
    The player will cut the deck near the bottom so that around 60 cards will not be used.
